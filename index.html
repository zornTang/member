Dr. Xiaopeng ZHANG PhD (2022.1)

Preparation of polymer microneedles and development of soluble insulin microneedles

I was born in Ningxia province and received my Ph.D. degree from Beijing University of Chemical Technology (BUCT) in December 2021. My Ph.D. study mainly focuses on the design, fabrication, and stability studies of polymer microneedles. Now, I'm working as a post-doctoral student in Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School. My research interests include nanobiotechnology, controlled drug delivery and microneedle for beauty or treatment of diseases such as cancer and diabetes.

Dr. Zohaib Hafiz Muhammad PhD (2025.6)

Design and application of coordination polymers and MOFs in drug delivery, molecular recognition, nucleotide structures, biomedical systems, and computational studies.

I was born in Rawalpindi, Pakistan and received my Ph.D. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, China in March 2025, and am now working as a postdoctoral fellow at Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School. I am working on coordination polymers and their crystal growth and design, and their various applications: drug delivery systems, amino acid separation and recognition, chiral drug recognition, the water helix structure of nucleotide coordination polymers, and metal-organic frameworks. I am also working on anticancer activity, antibacterial and antimicrobial peptide drug delivery systems, as well as MD simulations and theoretical calculations.

Xiaobin LI (2022.9)

Poly (β-amino ester) based drug delivery system for cancer therapy

My research focuses on the fabrication and function of pH-sensitive poly (β-amino ester) (PAE) and developing PAE-based targeted drug delivery systems via the bioengineering strategy to treat cancers and manage inflammation effectively with reduced side effects.
