Dr. Xiaopeng ZHANG PhD (2022.1)

Preparation of polymer microneedles and development of soluble insulin microneedles

I was born in Ningxia province and received my Ph.D. degree from Beijing University of Chemical Technology (BUCT) in December 2021. My Ph.D. study mainly focuses on the design, fabrication, and stability studies of polymer microneedles. Now, I'm working as a post-doctoral student in Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School. My research interests include nanobiotechnology, controlled drug delivery and microneedle for beauty or treatment of diseases such as cancer and diabetes.

Dr. Liang ZHAO PhD (2022.4)

Antibacterial polymer

I am a postdoc graduated from PSL (Paris Sciences & Lettres-PSL University) in chemistry. Due to the rapid rise in antibiotic resistance and emergence of multidrug-resistant bacteria. new strategies are required to develop new methods/materials that are able to prevent bacterial proliferation in health. The project is mainly working on the design and synthesis of pH-responsive nanoparticles via layer-by-layer assembly which is able to target lungs for improved therapy of acute lung inflammation/injury.

Junyu LIU (2021.9)

Metabolic Diseases, Animal Models, Pharmacokinetic Effects, Pu'er tea

My research direction is the efficacy and mechanism of natural products from food sources to improve glucose and lipid metabolism and construct related delivery systems. My research fields involve oral delivery system construction, the efficacy and mechanism of natural products in vitro and in vivo, bioinformatics analysis, etc. My cumulative IF of papers published as the first author or co-first author is 23.208.

Xiaobin LI (2022.9)

Poly (β-amino ester) based drug delivery system for cancer therapy

My research focuses on the fabrication and function of pH-sensitive poly (β-amino ester) (PAE) and developing PAE-based targeted drug delivery systems via the bioengineering strategy to treat cancers and manage inflammation effectively with reduced side effects.
